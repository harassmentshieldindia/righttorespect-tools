Rights Information Guide

What Recovery Agents Can and Cannot Do

Recovery agents must follow strict guidelines as per RBI regulations and Supreme Court directives:

  • They cannot use physical force or threaten you
  • They cannot use abusive language or make offensive gestures
  • They cannot contact you outside of permissible hours (7am to 7pm)
  • They cannot publicly humiliate you or disclose your debt to others
  • They cannot pretend to be government officials

Your Rights as a Borrower

You have specific rights when dealing with recovery agents:

  • Right to be treated with dignity and respect
  • Right to privacy regarding your financial matters
  • Right to file complaints against harassment
  • Right to legal representation
  • Right to be informed about your debt accurately

Complaint Filing Process

If you experience harassment, follow these steps:

  1. Document all incidents with dates, times, and details
  2. File a formal complaint with your bank first
  3. If unresolved within 30 days, escalate to Banking Ombudsman
  4. For criminal harassment, file a police complaint
  5. Consider legal action if harassment continues
