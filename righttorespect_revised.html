Your Legal Shield Against Harassment

These are not favors - these are your legal rights that banks and recovery agencies must respect

Protected Communication Hours

Recovery agents can ONLY contact you between 8:00 AM and 7:00 PM. Any calls outside these hours are clear violations you can report.

Privacy Protection

Your debt details cannot be disclosed to anyone - family, neighbors, employers, or colleagues - without your written consent.

No Abusive Language

You have the absolute right to be spoken to with respect and dignity. Threats, abuse, or intimidation are criminal offenses.

Home & Workplace Protection

Agents cannot enter your premises uninvited or harass you at your workplace. Your personal space is protected by law.

Written Communication Right

You can demand all communication in writing and refuse to engage in verbal harassment. This is your legal right.

Fair & Lawful Treatment

All recovery practices must be reasonable, fair, and legal. Banks cannot use any means they want - they have rules to follow.

Remember: These Are Not Requests - These Are Your Rights!
  • RBI Master Circular on Fair Practices Code - Binding guidelines all lenders must follow
  • Consumer Protection Act 2019 - Your shield against unfair practices
  • Indian Penal Code Sections 503, 506 - Criminal penalties for threats and intimidation
  • Banking Ombudsman Scheme - Free grievance mechanism against banks
